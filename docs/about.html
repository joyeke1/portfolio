About Me

Hello! I’m Joy Ekechukwu, an aspiring gameplay programmer. I’m passionate about video game development and always eager to learn new technologies!

Inspirations:

Favorite Show

Avatar: The Last Aribender & The Legend of Korra

I remember being so enthralled by this series at such a young age, the storytelling, the visuals, the music, and it's something that has stayed with me even now as I create my own stories.

Favorite Game

The Last of Us Part I & II

My first introduction to this game was back in 2014 when my cousin showed it to me on his PS3. I wasn't allowed to watch him play it then because of the scary content, however, I would sneak into the living room to watch him. 6 years later, my mom got me a PlayStation, and I was able to play the game myself. The bridge between the narrative storytelling and the amazing gameplay was what made me want to invest a career in video games.

Favorite Book

The Legend Series by Marie Lu

It's hard to define the best book I ever read, but when asked, my favorite, The Legend Series always comes to mind. I first read this series in middle school, then finished the 4th book in college when it came out later. I think this book introduced me to my love for sci-fi.

Favorite Movie

High School Musical Series

This one is a bit corny, but I've always been a fan of this series and had a huge crush on both Zac Efron and Vanessa Hudgens. I could recite every single song if you asked me to. (The best movie from the series is 3 though :3)
